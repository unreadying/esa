evan stover is a settler living on the home-land of the Tsleil Waututh Nation, the Kwikwetlem First Nation, and the general home of the Coast Salish People . he has spent the past four years studying advertising at ontario's college of art and design ( ocad ) , where he was mentored by genius advertisers like Christine Scott and Pi'ikea Clark . under the supervision of the two aformentioned advertiser-professors , stover completed an 'undergrad thesis' called 'the world needs more advertising' . stovers thesis quickly snowballed into an international collaborative pseudo concept agency called ad&ad which aims to bring big budget advertising to no budget businesses . evan stover is a versatile , untested , advertising oriented individual with considerable experience in design , programming , illustration , and writing . if you don't believe me , view his work right here on this webpage .
